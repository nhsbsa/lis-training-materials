What is your partner's NHS Number?
Where to find your partner's NHS Number

If your partner is registered with a GP, they'll have a 10-digit NHS Number that is unique to them.

It's usually on any letter or document your partner has received from the NHS, such as:

  • prescriptions, usually underneath their name and address
  • test results
  • appointment letters
  • maternity notes
  • an NHS medical card

Income and other money Which type of Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) do you get?

There are 2 types of ESA, income-related and contribution-based.

I'm not sure which type I get

Check your benefit payment letter

On the last page of your benefit letter, look under the heading 'Income and Benefits'.

It will say either:

  • 'your income-related entitlement is'
  • 'you are entitled to contribution-based'
